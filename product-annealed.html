Annealed Spring Strips are High Carbon Steel Strips. This type of steel has a very high yield strength. It allows objects made of this steel to return to their original shape despite significant bending or twisting after hardening and tempering.


It is usually available in soft annealed condition but can also be supplied in temper rolled hardness in range between 200-350 Hv (band range is +/- 40 Hv). This grade of steel is used where fatigue value and elastic limits are critical. It is also used where high strength, hardness and wear resistance is needed. Additionally, it also has good forming and blanking properties, however fairly poor drawing properties.
