Welcome to Steel Corporation

One Stop for Your Spring Steel Requirements - Cater to All Sizes


We are a family owned business established in 1972. Over the years, all partners and employees have put in great efforts to attain excellent reputation and credibility with customers and suppliers in India and overseas. Our focus has always been supplying quality material within set delivery timelines. Steel Corporation has evolved to meet the demands of the domestic and worldwide markets and now has various production and stockholding facilities.

We are committed to supply high quality strips to our customers and provide top tier service. Being an ISO 9001 certified company as well as a CRISIL rated company, we have ensured set procedure and standardized across all teams. We also have in house required testing facilities such as Rockwell, Vickers hardness testers, Spectrometer, Microscope, Muffle furnace and the latest Calibrated Dimensions Measuring Instrument.

EVERYTHING WE PRODUCE IS DESIGNED AND MADE TO ORDER.






COMPRESSION

The most common type of spring used in the engineering industry, coiled springs which are relatively easy to design and manufacture.



EXTENSION

Extension springs are closed-coiled springs used where a force is required to pull between two points.



TORSION

Torsion springs can be made from round or square wire, round is considerably cheaper and easier to manufacture.

Many of our springs operate in a 24/7 environment. With a complete range of materials in stock and a production team capable of meeting tight deadlines without compromising quality, we can offer the flexibility to respond to the most urgent of requirements – manufacturing and delivering on the same day if necessary. From prototyping and one-off development items to emergency assistance for breakdowns and large multi-batch production runs, Skegness Springs apply the same exacting standards at every stage of production. All the materials we use conform to recognised British, European or International standards and mechanical tests are performed throughout the production process. We work to design and tolerance values requested by our customer, or to standard specifications in BS1726, EN13906 and EN15800. We are able to supply printed load-test reports, material certificates and certificates of conformity when required.
