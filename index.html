Welcome to Steel Corporation

One Stop for Your Spring Steel Requirements - Cater to All Sizes


We are a family owned business established in 1972. Over the years, all partners and employees have put in great efforts to attain excellent reputation and credibility with customers and suppliers in India and overseas. Our focus has always been supplying quality material within set delivery timelines. Steel Corporation has evolved to meet the demands of the domestic and worldwide markets and now has various production and stockholding facilities.

We are committed to supply high quality strips to our customers and provide top tier service. Being an ISO 9001 certified company as well as a CRISIL rated company, we have ensured set procedure and standardized across all teams. We also have in house required testing facilities such as Rockwell, Vickers hardness testers, Spectrometer, Microscope, Muffle furnace and the latest Calibrated Dimensions Measuring Instrument.

EVERYTHING WE PRODUCE IS DESIGNED AND MADE TO ORDER.
