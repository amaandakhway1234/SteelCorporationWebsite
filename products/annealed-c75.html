ANNEALED C-75

ANNEALED C-75

C75 GRADE IS KNOWN AS SPRING STEEL STRIP ANNEALED, SPRING STEEL STRIP UNHARDENED, HIGH CARBON STEEL STRIP OR COLD ROLLED STEEL STRIP.

It offers a reasonably good fatigue value and elastic limit after hardening and tempering as compared to C65 and CK67 grades.
We supply for it against a specific request with a minimum order quantity and lead time for delivery depending on the requirement.
