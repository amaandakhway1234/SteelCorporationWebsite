ANNEALED C-80

ANNEALED C-80

THIS GRADE OF STEEL IS POPULARLY KNOWN AS SPRING STEEL STRIP ANNEALED, SPRING STEEL STRIP UNHARDENED, HIGH CARBON STEEL STRIP, COLD ROLLED STEEL STRIP.

This is a High Carbon cold rolled steel strip, with Spherodized grain structure, this steel is with a very high yield strength. This allows objects made of this steel to return to their original shape despite significant bending or twisting after hardening and tempering. Usually in soft annealed condition but can also be supplied in temper rolled hardness in range between 200-350 Hv (band range is+/- 40 Hv) These grade of steel is used where fatigue value and elastic limits are critical it is also used where high strength, hardness and wear resistant is needed. These steel has good forming and blanking properties but very poor drawing property.
